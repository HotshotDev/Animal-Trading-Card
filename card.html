  • Origin: China, mainly in Sichuan
  • Color: Black and White
  • Life Span: The life span of Giant pandas in the wild is approximately 20 years. Captive pandas may live to be 25-30 years old.
  • Birth Fact: Giant panda cubs are usually born in August or September and are the size of a stick of butter.

The giant panda has been on the endangered species list since 1990. The most significant threats to pandas are habitat loss and poaching.
